Indian Premier League

The Indian Premier League is a professional Twenty20 cricket league in India contested during March or April and May of every year by eight teams representing eight different cities in India.The league was founded by the Board of Control for Cricket in India in 2008.

Maximum Sixes, Fours & Wickets

2008 - 2019

Press right arrow or space for next slide.

Let us conclude it quickly

  • In 2008, when IPL initially started, because of new gaming format there used to be less power hittings showing the downward graph.

  • In most IPL seasons, the West Indies players used to show a great deal of power hitting. Players like Chris Gayle and Andre Russels used to top the most sixers chart.

  • Rishabh Pant is the only player in all IPL seasons who topped in both maximum sixes and maximum fours in 2018 IPL Season.

  • Pragyan Ojha just tool 21 wickets (lowest in all IPL seasons) but still topped the most wicket taker chart in 2010.

Distribution of Match Points Since Beginning

Toss Win and Match Win By Each Team

(2008 - 2019)

  • RCB aka Royal Challengers Banglore is the only team who has won half of their games winning the toss and lost the same number of games.
